Advanced Tensor G5 Performance

Powered by Google’s Tensor G5 chipset, the Pixel 10 delivers fast app launches, smooth multitasking, responsive performance in gaming, and efficient AI processing for photography and device features.

AI-Enhanced Triple Camera System

The Pixel 10 features a 48MP wide, 13MP ultrawide, and 10.8MP telephoto camera with up to 5× optical zoom and ~20× Super Res Zoom. With computational photography tools like Night Sight and Best Take, it captures sharp, detailed photos even in challenging lighting.

Vibrant 6.3″ Actua OLED Display

The 6.3-inch Actua OLED panel with FHD+ resolution and adaptive 120Hz refresh rate delivers bright, saturated colors and smooth scrolling — perfect for media, gaming, and daily usage.

The ~4970 mAh battery keeps you going throughout the day, with wired fast charging support plus magnetic Qi2 wireless charging capability for added convenience.
