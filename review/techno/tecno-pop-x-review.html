Smooth 120Hz Display

The Tecno Pop X features a 120Hz refresh rate display, which is quite impressive for a phone in this price range. Scrolling through apps, social media, or web pages feels noticeably smoother compared to typical budget phones. The large 6.75-inch screen also makes watching videos and browsing more comfortable. While the resolution is HD+, the higher refresh rate helps the phone feel more responsive in everyday use. For a budget device, this smooth display experience is definitely one of its standout features.

Long-Lasting 5000mAh Battery

The Tecno Pop X packs a 5000mAh battery, which is a big advantage for a budget smartphone. In everyday use like browsing, chatting, and watching videos, the phone can easily last a full day without much trouble. This makes it reliable for users who don’t want to worry about charging frequently. It also supports 15W charging, which is decent for this price range. While it may not charge extremely fast, the large battery ensures steady and dependable usage. Overall, battery life is one of the strongest points of the Tecno Pop X.

Android 15 and AI Features

The Tecno Pop X runs on Android 15 with Tecno’s HiOS interface, bringing a modern software experience to the budget segment. The interface feels clean and easy to navigate, making everyday tasks simple for most users. It also includes Ella AI, Tecno’s built-in assistant designed to help with basic smart functions. The system offers features like AI photo enhancements and useful smart tools that improve the overall usability of the phone. Animations and transitions feel smooth, especially when paired with the 120Hz display. For a budget smartphone, having the latest Android version helps the Tecno Pop X feel more up to date.

Price and Availability

The Tecno Pop X is positioned in the ultra-budget smartphone segment, making it an attractive option for users looking for modern features without spending too much. In India, the device is priced at around ₹8,499 for the base variant, which includes 4GB RAM and 64GB internal storage. With this pricing, Tecno aims to compete directly with other affordable smartphones in the under-₹9,000 category.

In terms of availability, the Tecno Pop X is expected to be available through online platforms such as Amazon , along with select offline retail stores across India. The smartphone officially launched on March 4, and sales are scheduled to begin shortly after the launch event. Buyers may also see introductory offers or bank discounts during the initial sale period, which could further reduce the effective price.
