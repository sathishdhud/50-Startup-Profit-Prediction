Final Verdict

Buy OnePlus 15T if: You want the best overall performance with the Snapdragon flagship processor, a very smooth 165Hz display, and a massive 7500 mAh battery with 100W fast charging. It also offers larger storage options up to 1TB and strong gaming performance, making it a great choice for power users and gamers looking for a balanced flagship at around ₹54,999.

Buy iQOO 15 Mini if: You prefer a more versatile camera system with triple 50MP sensors, including ultra-wide and telephoto lenses, along with strong gaming performance and a premium compact design. With a 7000 mAh battery and 144Hz AMOLED display, it is ideal for users who want a powerful phone with better camera flexibility at an expected price of around ₹49,999.

Overall: Both smartphones deliver flagship-level performance and large batteries in a compact design. The OnePlus 15T focuses more on raw performance, battery endurance, and storage options, while the iQOO 15 Mini stands out with its triple-camera system and balanced gaming features. Your choice should depend on whether you prioritize performance and battery life or camera versatility.
