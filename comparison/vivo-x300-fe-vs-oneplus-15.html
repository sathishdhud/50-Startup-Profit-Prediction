Final Verdict

Buy Vivo X300 FE if: You prefer a compact flagship phone with strong camera performance and a lightweight design. It offers a sharp 6.31-inch AMOLED display, a powerful processor, and fast 90W charging. With its expected competitive price and balanced flagship features, it is ideal for users who want a premium experience in a smaller form factor.

Buy OnePlus 15 if: You want maximum performance with the Snapdragon flagship chipset, a large 165Hz LTPO AMOLED display, and a huge 7300 mAh battery with ultra-fast charging. It also provides larger storage options and better gaming performance, making it a great choice for power users and heavy multitaskers.

Overall: Both smartphones offer flagship-level performance and premium features. The Vivo X300 FE focuses on camera quality, compact design, and balanced specifications, while the OnePlus 15 stands out with stronger performance, a larger display, and a bigger battery. The best choice depends on whether you prefer a compact camera-focused phone or a performance-focused flagship device.
