Final Verdict

Buy Realme C83 5G if: You want the longest possible battery life in this price segment. The phone packs a massive 7000 mAh battery and a smooth 144Hz display, making it ideal for users who prioritize endurance, smooth scrolling, and everyday usage without frequent charging.

Buy OPPO K13x 5G if: You prefer a more balanced smartphone with a better camera and faster charging. The device offers a 50MP main camera, 45W SUPERVOOC fast charging, and better durability features, making it a stronger option for photography and quicker daily charging.

Overall: Both smartphones target the budget 5G segment and share similar performance thanks to the MediaTek Dimensity 6300 chipset. The Realme C83 focuses on battery life and a smoother display experience, while the OPPO K13x provides a better camera system, faster charging, and stronger build quality. Your choice should depend on whether you prioritize battery endurance or camera and charging performance.
