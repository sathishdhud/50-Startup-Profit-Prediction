Frequently Asked Questions

Once your order is shipped, you will receive a tracking number via email or SMS. You can use this number on the respective courier's website to track your package. Alternatively, you can log in to your account on our website and check the status of your order in the "My Orders" section.

We offer a 30-day return policy for most products. Items must be unused, in the same condition you received them, and in the original packaging. To initiate a return, please contact our customer service team with your order number and reason for return.

Standard delivery typically takes 5-7 business days, while express delivery takes 2-3 business days. Delivery times may vary based on your location and the availability of the product. You can check the estimated delivery time for your specific location during the checkout process.
