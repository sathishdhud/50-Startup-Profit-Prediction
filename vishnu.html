Who We Are

Creating Enduring Value Through Responsible Development

Hasini Estates Pvt. Ltd. is a purpose-driven real estate development company committed to creating thoughtfully planned communities that balance modern infrastructure with nature, well-being, and long-term prosperity.

Founded on the belief that real estate should offer more than ownership, Hasini Estates focuses on developing vibrant, green, and self-sustaining gated communities where families can truly belong—not just invest.

With a strong foundation of trust, transparency, and regulatory compliance, every Hasini Estates project is developed with clear titles, vastu-compliant layouts, and future-ready infrastructure.

Leadership & Founder's Vision

Guided by Purpose, Driven by Experience

Hasini Estates is led by Ravi Kuncharapu, a seasoned entrepreneur with deep experience across technology, mining, and infrastructure development in India and the USA.

His return to India was driven by a singular purpose: to build real estate communities that people can emotionally connect with—not just financially invest in.

This philosophy defines every Hasini Estates project, where quality, transparency, and customer satisfaction are at the forefront of everything we do.
