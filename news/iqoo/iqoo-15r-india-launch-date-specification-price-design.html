iQOO 15R Roundup: India Launch Date, Specifications, Expected Price and More

By Dhuda Sathish • Reviewed by Maaney Editorial Team • Updated
Key Highlights
India launch: iQOO 15R is confirmed to debut in India on February 24.
Performance: Powered by the flagship Snapdragon 8 Gen 5 chipset.
Display: 6.59-inch AMOLED panel with up to 144Hz refresh rate.
Battery: Massive 7,600mAh battery with fast charging support.
Why it matters: The iQOO 15R aims to deliver flagship-level performance and battery life at a more competitive price point in India.
Key Highlights
  • Device: iQOO 15R
  • Launch: February 24 (India)
  • Chipset: Snapdragon 8 Gen 5
  • Display: AMOLED, 144Hz
  • Battery: 7,600mAh

The upcoming iQOO 15R is shaping up to be one of the most performance-focused smartphones in its segment, with the company officially confirming its India launch timeline.

Based on official teasers, retailer listings, and industry reports, the iQOO 15R is expected to combine flagship-grade hardware with a large battery and a high-refresh-rate display, targeting power users and gamers.

At the core of the iQOO 15R is Qualcomm's Snapdragon 8 Gen 5 processor, which is expected to deliver significant gains in gaming performance, AI tasks, and sustained efficiency compared to previous-generation chips.

The phone is tipped to feature a 6.59-inch AMOLED display with a 144Hz refresh rate, offering smoother animations, better responsiveness, and improved visual clarity for gaming and multimedia consumption.

iQOO 15R is expected to focus on performance, display quality, and long battery life.

One of the biggest highlights is the rumored 7,600mAh battery, which could place the iQOO 15R among the longest-lasting performance smartphones in its category, supported by fast charging technology.

While official pricing has not been announced yet, reports suggest the iQOO 15R will be positioned under the ₹50,000 mark, making it a strong contender in the premium mid-range segment.
