AI+ Pulse 2 Launched in India with 6000mAh Battery, 50MP AI Camera and 120Hz Display Under ₹7,000

By Dhuda Sathish • Reviewed by Maaney Editorial Team • Updated March 2, 2026
Highlights
  • Launched in India at ₹5,999 introductory price
  • Powered by Unisoc T7250 octa-core chipset
  • Runs Android 16 with NxtQuantum OS
  • Massive 6000mAh battery with 18W fast charging
  • 6.75-inch HD+ display with 120Hz refresh rate
  • 50MP AI dual rear camera setup
  • 8MP front camera for selfies and video calls
  • Sale begins March 11 via Flipkart
Key Highlights
  • India launch price ₹5,999
  • Unisoc T7250 processor
  • Android 16 out of the box
  • 6000mAh battery with 18W charging
  • 120Hz HD+ display
  • 50MP AI rear camera
  • Sale from March 11 on Flipkart

AI+ has officially launched the Pulse 2 smartphone in India, targeting the ultra-budget segment with aggressive pricing and solid specifications. The device comes powered by the Unisoc T7250 octa-core processor and is positioned as a value-focused upgrade over the previous Pulse model. The launch was confirmed through Flipkart listings and official announcements ahead of its first sale.

AI+ Pulse 2 runs on Android 16 with the brand’s NxtQuantum OS on top, offering a clean and updated software experience in the entry-level category. The smartphone packs a massive 6000mAh battery with 18W fast charging support, a smooth 120Hz HD+ display, and a 50MP AI-powered dual rear camera setup. With an introductory price of ₹5,999, AI+ aims to disrupt the sub-₹7,000 smartphone market in India.

The AI+ Pulse 2 comes with a 6.75-inch HD+ display featuring a 120Hz refresh rate, which is quite impressive in the ultra-budget category. The higher refresh rate ensures smoother scrolling, better animation transitions, and an improved overall user experience for daily usage and casual gaming.

Powering the smartphone is the Unisoc T7250 octa-core chipset, designed to handle everyday tasks like browsing, social media, video streaming, and light gaming efficiently. The processor is paired with up to 6GB RAM and 128GB storage, offering smooth multitasking performance at an affordable price point.

One of the biggest highlights of the AI+ Pulse 2 is its massive 6000mAh battery, which promises extended usage without frequent charging. The device supports 18W fast charging via USB Type-C, helping users quickly top up the battery when needed.

In terms of camera capabilities, the smartphone features a 50MP AI-powered dual rear camera setup, aimed at delivering detailed daylight photos with improved color accuracy. On the front, it houses an 8MP selfie camera suitable for video calls and social media usage.

The AI+ Pulse 2 runs on Android 16 with NxtQuantum OS on top, bringing updated software features to the entry-level segment. The clean interface and optimized system experience aim to provide better stability and smoother performance.

With an introductory price starting at ₹5,999 and sales beginning March 11 via Flipkart, AI+ is positioning the Pulse 2 as a strong contender in the sub-₹7,000 smartphone market in India. Its combination of large battery capacity, high refresh rate display, and 50MP camera makes it one of the most aggressively priced devices in this category for 2026.
