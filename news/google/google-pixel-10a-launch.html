Google Pixel 10a Launched in India: 5,100mAh Battery and 48MP Camera Bring Big Upgrades

By Dhuda Sathish • Updated Maaney Editorial •
Key Highlights
India launch: Pixel 10a officially launched on February 18, 2026.
Price: ₹49,999 for 8GB RAM + 256GB storage.
Battery: Massive 5,100mAh unit, biggest in Pixel A-series.
Camera: 48MP primary sensor with Google AI imaging.
Why it matters: Pixel 10a brings flagship-grade battery life, AI features, and long software support at a mid-range price point.
Key Highlights
  • Launch: Feb 18, 2026
  • Price: ₹49,999
  • Battery: 5,100mAh
  • Camera: 48MP rear
  • Chip: Tensor G4

Google has officially launched the Pixel 10a in India, expanding its mid-range smartphone lineup with notable upgrades in battery capacity, camera hardware, and long-term software support.

The Pixel 10a continues Google’s A-series strategy of delivering a flagship-like experience at a more accessible price, while focusing on real-world usability, AI-powered features, and clean Android performance.

One of the biggest upgrades on the Pixel 10a is its 5,100mAh battery, making it the largest battery ever fitted to a Pixel A-series smartphone. Google claims improved power efficiency when paired with the new Tensor G4 chipset.

On the camera front, the Pixel 10a features a 48-megapixel primary sensor supported by Google’s advanced computational photography. Users can expect improved low-light shots, enhanced portrait processing, and smarter AI-assisted imaging.

Google Pixel 10a introduces a new 48MP sensor paired with Pixel AI camera features.

The smartphone is powered by the Tensor G4 processor, enabling on-device AI tasks, smoother performance, and tighter integration with Google’s ecosystem. The Pixel 10a ships with the latest Android version and is promised up to seven years of updates.

With a competitive price of ₹49,999, the Pixel 10a directly targets users looking for reliable cameras, long software support, and strong battery life — areas where many mid-range phones compromise.

Google’s focus with the Pixel 10a appears clear: practical AI, dependable performance, and longevity rather than flashy specifications alone.
