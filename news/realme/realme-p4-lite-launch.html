Realme P4 Lite 4G Confirmed to Launch in India on February 20 with Massive 6,300mAh Battery

Feb 16, 2026 Dhuda Sathish 5 Min Read

Realme has officially confirmed the India launch of its latest budget smartphone, the Realme P4 Lite 4G. The device is set to debut on February 20 and will focus on long battery life, a slim form factor, and essential everyday performance. Positioned as the most affordable model in the P4 lineup, the P4 Lite 4G aims to attract users looking for reliability over flagship features.

Design and Build: Slim Profile with a Focus on Durability

Realme highlights the slim design of the P4 Lite 4G despite its large 6,300mAh battery.

Despite packing a 6,300mAh battery, Realme claims the P4 Lite 4G maintains a relatively slim profile, making it comfortable for daily use. The official teaser images suggest a clean rear panel design with subtle branding and a practical finish aimed at durability.

The smartphone is also confirmed to feature IP54-rated dust and splash resistance, adding an extra layer of protection for everyday environments — a feature rarely seen in this price segment.

Battery Life: The Key Highlight of the Realme P4 Lite 4G

Battery life is the standout feature of the Realme P4 Lite 4G. With its massive 6,300mAh battery, the phone is expected to deliver extended screen-on time, comfortably lasting more than a full day on a single charge for most users.

Realme has positioned the device for users who prioritize endurance — including students, casual gamers, and first-time smartphone buyers — over cutting-edge hardware specifications.

Performance, Software, and Everyday Experience

The Realme P4 Lite 4G is designed for smooth everyday usage, including social media, video streaming, browsing, and light multitasking. It will run on Realme UI based on Android, offering a familiar interface with useful customization options and system optimizations.

Realme has also confirmed the inclusion of AI-powered features such as call noise reduction and smart system enhancements, improving the overall user experience without compromising efficiency.

Launch Date and Availability in India

The Realme P4 Lite 4G will officially launch in India on February 20. Pricing and sale details are expected to be revealed during the launch event.

Once announced, the device is likely to be available through Realme’s official website and leading online retail platforms. The phone is expected to target the entry-level and budget segment.
