POCO X8 Pro & X8 Pro Max Leak Reveals Design and Key Specs Ahead of Launch

Leaked renders: POCO X8 Pro and X8 Pro Max design images surface online.

The upcoming POCO X8 Pro and X8 Pro Max have surfaced in fresh leaks, revealing key design elements and early hardware details ahead of their expected global launch.

According to multiple reports, the leaked renders showcase POCO’s evolving design language while hinting at notable upgrades in display technology, performance hardware, and battery capacity across the X8 Pro lineup.

The POCO X8 Pro series is expected to feature OLED displays with a high refresh rate, offering smoother visuals and improved brightness compared to its predecessor.

Performance is likely to be powered by MediaTek Dimensity processors, positioning the X8 Pro models as performance-centric options in the competitive mid-range category.

Leaked renders hint at design refinements and hardware upgrades in the POCO X8 Pro series.

The X8 Pro Max, in particular, is rumored to feature a significantly larger battery, which could make it one of the most endurance-focused POCO smartphones to date.

While POCO has not officially confirmed specifications yet, the growing number of leaks suggests that an official announcement may not be far off.
