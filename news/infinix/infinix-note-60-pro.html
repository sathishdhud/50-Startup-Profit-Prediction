Infinix Note 60 Pro India Launch Expected Soon: 144Hz AMOLED, Snapdragon 7s Gen 4 & 90W Fast Charging

Feb 11, 2026 Editorial Team 7 Min Read

Infinix is gearing up to expand its popular Note lineup with the upcoming Infinix Note 60 Pro. Based on official teasers and early retail indicators, the smartphone is expected to launch in India soon, bringing premium display technology, flagship-grade charging speeds, and a refined design language to the mid-range segment.

Refined Design with Rear Matrix Display

The Infinix Note 60 Pro introduces a fresh visual identity for the Note series. Teaser images reveal a clean rear panel with a bold camera island that houses a Matrix-style secondary display. This rear screen is designed to show notifications, charging status, and system alerts, adding both style and functionality.

Official teasers highlight the Matrix rear display and leather-textured finish.

Expected Color Options

Infinix is expected to offer the Note 60 Pro in bold yet premium colorways. These finishes focus on durability, reduced fingerprints, and a distinctive look aimed at younger users and power consumers.

Snapdragon Performance Upgrade

Powering the Note 60 Pro is the Snapdragon 7s Gen 4 chipset, marking a strategic shift for Infinix. Built for sustained performance and efficiency, the processor is paired with advanced vapor chamber cooling, ensuring stable gaming and multitasking even under heavy workloads.

AI Features & Smart Enhancements

Infinix is expected to introduce smarter system-level AI features with the Note 60 Pro. These include AI-assisted image processing, battery health optimization, adaptive performance tuning, and intelligent notification handling via the rear Matrix display.

Expected Specifications

  • Display: 6.78-inch 1.5K AMOLED, 144Hz refresh rate
  • Processor: Qualcomm Snapdragon 7s Gen 4
  • Rear Camera: 50MP Night Master primary sensor
  • Battery: 6500mAh with 90W wired fast charging
  • Audio: JBL-tuned dual stereo speakers
  • Software: Android 15-based XOS

Camera & Low-Light Imaging

The Note 60 Pro focuses heavily on night photography. Its 50MP Night Master camera is optimized with enhanced noise reduction and AI scene detection, promising clearer low-light photos and improved HDR output for both photos and videos.
