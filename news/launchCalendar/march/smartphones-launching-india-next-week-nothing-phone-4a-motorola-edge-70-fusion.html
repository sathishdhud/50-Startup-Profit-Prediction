1. Nothing Phone (4a) – Launching in India on March 5, 2026

Key Launch Highlights

  • Official India launch scheduled for March 5, 2026.
  • Expected to feature a 6.7-inch AMOLED display with 120Hz refresh rate.
  • Powered by a Qualcomm Snapdragon chipset for smooth daily performance.
  • Rumored to include a 50MP primary camera with improved image processing.
  • Equipped with a 5000mAh battery with fast charging support.
  • Signature transparent design with upgraded Glyph lighting system.

The Nothing Phone (4a) is set to launch in India next week and is expected to compete in the premium mid-range segment. With its transparent back panel, refined Glyph interface, and balanced hardware, the device aims to attract users looking for a stylish yet performance-focused 5G smartphone in March 2026.

Key Launch Highlights

  • Expected to launch in India on March 6, 2026.

  • Powered by a Qualcomm Snapdragon 7-series processor.
  • Equipped with a 50MP Sony primary camera sensor.
  • Backed by a massive 7000mAh battery with fast charging support.
  • Likely to be available via Flipkart and Motorola’s official store.

The Motorola Edge 70 Fusion is set to debut in India next week and is expected to target users looking for a performance-focused 5G smartphone with a large battery and high refresh rate display. With its curved AMOLED panel and upgraded camera hardware, the device aims to strengthen Motorola’s position in the competitive mid-range segment.

Full specifications, pricing details, and availability will be confirmed during the official launch event. The smartphone is expected to compete directly with other upcoming March 2026 launches in the ₹20,000 to ₹30,000 segment.
